Katie Couric is an American journalist, presenter, producer, and author.

She is the founder of Katie Couric Media, a multimedia news and production company. She also publishes a daily newsletter, Wake Up Call.

Katie Couric has been married twice. Her first husband Jay Monahan with whom she shares two children, Ellie Monahan and Caroline Couric Monahan died in 1998. She is currently married to Chicago financier, John Molner.

Ellie Monahan

Ellie Monahan is the first daughter of Katie Couric and her late husband Jay Monahan.

She was born on July 23, 1991 in Washington, D.C., United States.

Ellie Monahan is an American writer and director.

31-year-old Ellie is known for her work on The Boys (2019), Liked (2017), Grams (2016), and Mr. Robot (2017).

Ellie Monahan has been married to Mark Dobrosky since 2021.

Caroline Couric Monahan

Caroline Couric Monahan is the youngest daughter of Katie Couric and Jay Monahan.

She was born on January 5, 1996 in New York, United States.

Caroline graduated from Stanford in 2018 and spent time teaching English in France.

She received a Master of Science in Journalism from Columbia University in 2019

Caroline worked as a Fact Check Producer at Reuters in New York from February 2020 to May 2021.

She currently works as an Associate Producer at Ark Media in Brooklyn, New York, United States.

Caroline Couric Monahan is currently 26 years old.
