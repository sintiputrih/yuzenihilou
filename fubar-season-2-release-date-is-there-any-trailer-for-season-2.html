FUBAR Season 2 Release Date: Is There Any Trailer for Season 2?

January 2024 · 4 minute read

FUBAR Season 2 Release Date: Arnold Schwarzenegger stars in the spy comedy series about a father and daughter who work for the CIA without knowing each other. When they find out, they have to figure out how to solve their problems (and the case).

Arnold Schwarzenegger’s first scripted lead part in a TV show is in FUBAR, which is exciting because we’ve watched him kick ass in some of the best action movies for years. By the way, FUBAR means “Fucked Up Beyond All Repair/Recognition.”

But will there be more after the first season? Here’s what we know about when season 2 of FUBAR might come out. Spoilers for the Netflix show are coming up.

FUBAR Season 2 Release Date

The second season of FUBAR was publicly announced on June 17, 2023. However, Luke and Emma won’t be back on our screens until at least late 2024.

The ongoing 2023 Writers Strike is messing up production plans all over Hollywood, and the Screen Actors Guild’s possible decision to join them on the picket line could also delay some of our favorite TV shows.

Is There Any Trailer for Season 2 of FUBAR?

We don’t expect to see a video for FUBAR season 2 until about a month before the show starts.

On April 19, 2023, just over a month before the first shows came out, Netflix put out a full season 1 trailer. We expect the same thing if there is a second season. In the meantime, you can watch Netflix’s fun ‘Arnold Schwarzenegger Is Back, Baby!’ video or watch the best Arnold Schwarzenegger movies again.

FUBAR Season 2 Cast Speculation

Arnold Schwarzenegger and Monica Barbaro will be back in season 2 as Luke and Emma Brunner, respectively. The rest of the FUBAR group will also be back.

What Happens in FUBAR Season 2?

The storyline of FUBAR season 2 will probably be about what happens after the team’s cover is blown at the wedding at the season’s end.

In the first season, Luke and Emma find out that they are both CIA spies. Luke is asked to come out of retirement. When they start working together, there are some misunderstandings and relationship problems that need to be worked out. Over time, Emma learns to value what she’s learned from her father, and Luke learns to accept Emma’s skills. It was a nice twist on the normal way two cops work together.

But the ending caused chaos: everyone’s secret is out, and the CIA sends a van to get them away from danger.

We think that by season 2, they will have formed a strong team and will be able to solve new problems with the help of Roo and the rest of the gang. They will also continue to build their personal and professional relationships while dealing with the fallout from blowing their covers.

Where Can I Watch Season 2 of FUBAR?

If season 2 of FUBAR came out, you’d be able to watch it on Netflix. Only the video service can show the show.

Netflix probably wants people to think that the show is one of their best comedies so far. If that happens, you can expect all of season 2’s episodes to drop there at once when a new part comes out.

While waiting for the premiere of FUBER season 2, you can watch more shows in the same genre. Some examples of such shows are School Spirits Season 2 and Transplant Season 4.

Arnold Schwarzenegger stars in the spy comedy series FUBAR, which follows a father and daughter working for the CIA without knowing each other.
