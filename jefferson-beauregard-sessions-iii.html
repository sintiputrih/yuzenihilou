Career

Professionally, Jeff is a politician and attorney. From the year 2017 to 2018, he served as the 84th United States Attorney General. Previously, from 1997 to 2017, he served as the United States Senator from Alabama. Therefore, it was before resigning from that position to serve in the administration of President Donald Trump.

Similarly, from 1981 to 1993, Jeff served as the U.S. Attorney for the Southern District of Alabama. In 1994, Jeff was elected Attorney General of Alabama. Similarly, in the year 1996, Jeff was elected to the U.S. Senate and was re-elected in the years 2002, 2008, and 2014.

Jeff was considered one of the most conservative Senators during his Senate tenure. Jeff was an early supporter of Donald Trump’s 2016 presidential campaign. Therefore, he got nominations from Trump for the post of U.S. Attorney General. In February 2017, Jeff was confirmed and sworn in as Attorney General.

Moreover, in his confirmation hearings, Jeff stated under oath that he did not have contact with Russian officials during the 2016 presidential campaign. And, also that Jeff was unaware of any contact between Trump campaign members and Russian officials.
