Kate Middleton Wears Purple Gucci Blouse to Visit Children's Center

April 2024 · 2 minute read
  • Kate Middleton opted for an elegant purple Gucci blouse and Jigsaw wide-leg trousers for a visit to Henry Fawcett Children’s Centre today.
  • The Duchess of Cambridge learned about LEAP, a program supporting parents, their children, and caretakers, according to Kensington Palace.
  • Kate, a mom of three, had plenty of adorable moments meeting other mothers and playing with kids.

After going for a bold red ladylike ensemble at Westminster Abbey yesterday, Kate Middleton changed up her style with a chic Gucci pussy-bow blouse (in regal purple) and black wide leg pants by Jigsaw this morning. She finished off her simply elegant look with black pointed-toe pumps and a lilac Aspinal of London handle bag.

The Duchess of Cambridge stepped out for a visit to London's Henry Fawcett Children’s Centre, where she learned about how the parenting program LEAP is "using the Parent and Infant Relationship Service (PAIRS) to support parents, carers and their children," Kensington Palace announced.

REX/Shutterstock

Neil Mockford//Getty Images

During her trip, Kate got to meet fellow parents and their adorable young children during a session called "Together Time," which supports "parents’ ability to understand their child’s cues and feel more confident in their parenting," according to the Palace.

The duchess herself is a mother to three royal kids: Prince George, 5, Princess Charlotte, 3, and Prince Louis, who will turn one next month. Although her brood wasn't in tow today, she had a number of sweet moments with babies and toddlers during her visit.

When recently asked about having baby number four, Kate, who just gave birth to her youngest last April, didn't seem so enthusiastic. "I think William would be a little worried," she joked.

She is, however, looking forward to Prince Harry and Meghan Markle's baby. "It's such a special time to have little kiddies," she told a fan in Leicester in November. "And now a cousin for George, Charlotte and Louis as well! It'll be really special."
