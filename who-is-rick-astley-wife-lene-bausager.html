This report will highlight Rick Astley’s wife, Lene Bausager. Before doing that, let’s get to know Rick in perspective. Rick Astley, born Richard Paul Astley, is an English singer who has been on the music scene for several decades.

He was born on February 6, 1966, in Newton-le-Willows, Lancashire, England. He gained worldwide fame in the 1980s, having multiple hits, including his signature song “Never Gonna Give You Up,” which was released in 1987 and became a massive success, reaching number one on the charts in 25 countries.

He returned to music full-time in the 2000s after a 6-year hiatus. Outside of his music career, Astley has occasionally worked as a radio DJ and a podcaster.

Who is Rick Astley Wife?

Rick Astley is married to Lene Bausager. They have been married since 2003. Lene Bausager is a Danish film producer with an impressive career. Among her notable works are her contributions to esteemed films like “Cashback” in 2005, “The Broken” in 2008, and “Ginger & Rosa” in 2012.

Her film “cashback” was so big that, it earned her a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Live-Action Short Film in 2006.

Despite her involvement in the film industry, Lene has left her mark on the music world. She notably produced the music videos for Madonna’s hit track “Hung Up” in 2005 and her husband’s melodic creation, “She Makes Me,” released in 2018.

Rick and Lene’s story began in 1987, when they first crossed paths. Working in marketing at his Danish record label, Lene caught his attention, leading to their blossoming relationship in 1989. They celebrate over 35 years of togetherness, having been married for more than two decades.

They share a strong commitment and a strong relationship. Together, they have a daughter named Emilie Astley, born in 1992.
