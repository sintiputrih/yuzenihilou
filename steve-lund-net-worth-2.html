Steve Lund Wiki: Salary, Married, Wedding, Spouse, Family

Steven J. Lund (born October 30, 1953) is an American businessman. He is the co-founder and the current executive chairman of Nu Skin Enterprises. Lund has also served as the CEO of Nu Skin. Lund also founded Eli Publishing in 1997.Lund considers Mitt Romney a close friend, and donated $3 million to Restore Our Future, a super PAC that supported Romney in 2012. Lund is a fiscal and social conservative who has also given money to the Republican National Committee and Republican Congressman (and former Nu Skin Employee) Jason Chaffetz.Lund is a member of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints, and has served as the president of the Atlanta mission of the The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (LDS Church). He currently serves on the Fifth Quorum of the Seventy. Lund collects religious paintings, and owns an original copy of the Book of Mormon.Lund lives in Provo, Utah with his wife, Kalleen. He and his wife have four children. Lund received a BA and a JD from Brigham Young University. ﻿﻿﻿﻿﻿
