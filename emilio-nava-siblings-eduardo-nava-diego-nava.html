Emilio Nava siblings: Eduardo Nava, Diego Nava

How many siblings does Emilio Nava have?

Nava has two brothers.

Who are Emilio Nava siblings?

Emilio Nava was born to former Olympians Eduardo Nava and Xóchitl Escobedo. His father competed in the men’s 100 meters at the 1988 Summer Olympics. His mother represented Mexico at the 1988 Summer Olympics in Seoul, South Korea.

He has two brothers named Eduardo and Diego who like him, are tennis players. His eldest brother and his father’s namesake, Eduardo played college tennis at TCU and Wake Forest University. He has a career-high ATP singles ranking of 792 and a career-high ATP doubles ranking of 911.

His other brother, Diego, who previously enjoyed soccer and track and field meets until he was 12, decided to focus on tennis.

Does Emilio Nava share the same parents with his siblings?

The siblings all share the same parents.
