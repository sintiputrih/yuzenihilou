Who is Xandra Pohl? Fans speculate Alix Earle and budding influencer had a fallout

August 2024 · 4 minute read

FLORIDA, MIAMI: Alexandra Pohl, widely recognized as Xandra Pohl, has gained prominence as a TikTok sensation. Her breakthrough moment came when she started sharing 'GRWM' (Get Ready With Me) videos on the platform, amassing millions of views. She later expanded her career into DJing, establishing herself as a notable figure. Additionally, she is well known for her close friendship with Alix Earle.

Alix Earle, too, found fame through TikTok during her time as a student at the University of Miami. Since graduating, both Earle and Xandra Pohl have built substantial social media following. However, the absence of joint posts of Earle and Xandra Pohl, has led to speculation among fans about the state of their friendship, with some assuming significant discord between the pair.

Why did Alix Earle and Xandra Pohl’s fans speculate about their friendship?

Since Alix Earle and Xandra Pohl’s graduation earlier this year, the two have continued to expand their presence in the digital sphere. Earle has transitioned into a prominent 'it girl' influencer, while Xandra has focused on her DJing career.

Given their simultaneous rise in the same industry, fans have long compared Earle and Xandra Pohl, speculating about the state of their friendship when they go without posting together for extended periods. There have been assumptions of significant "drama" between the two.

Currently, several months after graduation, fans are even more convinced that something may be amiss in Earle and Xandra's friendship, as the two independently take on New York Fashion Week this week. The initial indication of a potential disagreement between the two emerged during their graduation ceremony in May 2023 at the University of Miami. Xandra was notably absent from Alix's graduation photo session with her circle of friends.

These speculations seemed to dissipate when Alix prominently featured in Xandra's photo collection, appearing in three separate snapshots from the ceremony. The images captured their shared laughter and lighthearted antics, dispelling any doubts about their bond.

"Freshman Alix and Xandra would be delighted to see how far we've come," Alix playfully remarked on Xandra's post.

Following their graduation, Alix and Xandra embarked on a European adventure, accompanied by a close-knit circle of university friends.

The questions about their friendship came up again when Alix and Xandra spent most of the summer in The Hamptons but didn't seem to meet up or post pictures together. Even though they still liked and shared each other's posts on social media, Alix and Xandra haven't left comments on each other's Instagram pictures for about eight weeks.

The last time Xandra left a comment on Alix's post was on July 11, and Alix's last comment on Xandra's post was on July 13.
