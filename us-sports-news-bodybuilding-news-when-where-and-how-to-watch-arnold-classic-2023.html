When, Where and How to Watch Arnold Classic 2023?

March 2024 · 3 minute read

The first week of March is all set to be one of the most exciting weeks for fitness fans across the world. Arnold Classic 2023 has the bodybuilding community in a chokehold this year. Some of the top contenders have been promising, and fans look forward to seeing them in a new and improved form. With names like Big Ramy, Nick Walker, and Shaun Clarida, everyone is hyped about the upcoming championship and what the contenders might bring to the table.

This year, the Arnold Classic championship takes place in Columbus, Ohio. But one doesn’t have to travel all the way to Columbus to attend the Arnold Sports Festival. Fans can now access the events and watch their favorite bodybuilders show off their gains from the comfort of their own homes. Wondering how? Here are the details.

Watch the Arnold Classic 2023 from anywhere around the world

The bodybuilding community across the world can now buy tickets to the Arnold Classic without having to travel to the event location. The championship offers pay-per-view packages, where fans can access the live telecast of the show and other perks. The schedule for the competition is out, and fans have marked their calendars for March 3rd and 4th to watch their favorite competitors battle it out.

The schedule for the competition is as follows

  • March 3rd (7 PM – 9.30 PM) marks the beginning of the Arnold Classic, with a prejudging round. The IFBB Pro League Meet & Greet event will be held at Columbus Convention Center, Battelle Grand.
  • March 4th (7:00 PM – 10:00 PM) marks the final round for the show.

While these rounds are conducted at the Columbus Convention Center, those with pay-per-view access will get a good view of the competition.

The pay-per-view service is offered by fanmio.com where fans can stream Arnold Classic on any of their devices. Viewers get to select a suitable package for themselves in order to get access to the show. These packages offer other perks like signed merchandise in a bundle. There are also early access packages that could save some bucks without missing out on the same perks. Offers include signed photographs by Arnold Schwarzenegger and Arnold Classic 2023 T-shirts.

Who will make an appearance this year at the Classic?

Recently, the Arnold Sports Festival announced the star-studded roster of the Arnold Classic 2023. Participants who made it big in Mr. Olympia 2022, along with more promising names, have made an appearance on the list of participants this year. From the 10 men competing for the title, certain names have outshone the others due to their fanbase.

Fans have set their eyes on Big Ramy and Nick Walker, for starters. After their stint at Mr. Olympia 2022, where both contenders made it to the top 5, fans have their hopes up for both of them. Apart from that, the community is also keeping a watch on Samson Dauda, William Bonac, and Shaun Clarida who are some of the popular names in the bodybuilding fraternity. Coming March, things might get more and more exciting once the contestants get up on stage.
