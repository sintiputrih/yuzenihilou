Who is Justine Lupe?

Justine Lupe is an actress of American nationality. Justine Lupe is popular for her appearance in Mr. Mercedes, Succession, Cristela, The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel, etc.

In 2011, she stepped into the screen industry and has done many screen projects. Recently, this artist did the character of Nell Rutherford in 2022’s mystery-thriller movie Luckiest Girl Alive.

As a director/producer, she has worked in South of Bix (2019’s Short) and The Recorder (2021’s Short). She has written 10 episodes of the TV mini-series The Non-Essentials.
