Gabrielle Union Recalls Advising Ayesha And Steph Curry To End Things Back When They Started Dating

January 2024 · 2 minute read

Gabrielle Union had some advice for Ayesha and Steph Curry when they first started dating and that was to break up! Thankfully, they obviously ignored that because today, the two are very happily married with three beautiful kids!

Now, looking back, Gabrielle recalled what she had to say at the time and how she feels now!

She, her hubby, Dwyane Wade and the Currys had a little IG Live hangout and that is when the topic came up.

Gabrielle revealed how at first, she told the couple that they just had to end things!

‘I was like, ‘You guys, the likelihood of this working out is very low and you should just break up now and have sex with other people,’’ Gabrielle said as the group laughed and drank some wine on stream.

She went on to add: ‘I used a little bit more different language, but is that not what I told y’all both?’

Ayesha then recalled how ‘You looked at me and said, ‘How old are you? OK.’’

The laughter went on, Union jokingly saying how she thought that ‘precisely’ because she was a ‘thot!’

Then, her husband joined in on the fun, apologizing in the name of the Wade family and stressing that it’s ‘not who we are anymore.’

At the end of the day, however, they were messing around while remembering the past, but Union expressed how glad she actually was that the Currys did not follow her piece of advice.

I have known you guys over a decade and there are a lot of people who talk about faith and there is a lot of people who talk about moving the culture forward, but something is not real somewhere. And when people are like, ‘Are they real?’ I’m like, ‘yes!’’
