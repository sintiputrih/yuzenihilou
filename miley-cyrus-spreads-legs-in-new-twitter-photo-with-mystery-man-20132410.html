Miley Cyrus Spreads Legs, Poses With Mystery Man: Raunchy New Photo

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Miley Cyrus‘ most shocking fashion move yet happened at the 30th Annual Night of Stars event on Oct. 22, when she swapped the fishnets and see-through shirts for an elegant Marc Jacobs aqua sequin-covered gown. 

Cyrus, 20, was a presenter at the event, and tweeted about it on Oct. 23 saying, “I got to give the biggest superstars in the room a pretty rad award. Congrats to Robert and Marc @MarcJacobsIntl.” 

And though she was turning heads for all the right reasons at the event, Cyrus quickly went back to her old ways later that night, swapping her classy, floor-length gown for a short black romper, flannel shirt worn as a sweater belt, fishnet tights, and black platform sneakers. 

The “Wrecking Ball” singer went to the Beatrice Inn — a celeb-beloved restaurant and cocktail bar in downtown Manhattan — and from the looks of a new Twitter photo she posted in the same outfit, she went out clubbing later that night. 

“So it’s true. If you turn the lights off and look into a mirror and say #turndown4WHAT 3 times fast shit gets TURNT,” she tweeted along with a suggestive photo. 

In the picture, the pop star is sitting on a countertop with her legs spread open. A bearded man with a blurred out face is standing between her legs wearing a leather jacket. The twerk-happy singer is holding dollar bills, flicking off the camera, and her infamous tongue is hanging out.
