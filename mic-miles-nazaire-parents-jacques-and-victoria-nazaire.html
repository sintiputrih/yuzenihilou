Meet MIC Miles Nazaire Parents: Jacques And Victoria Nazaire!

September 2024 · 6 minute read

Victoria Nazaire and Jacques Nazaire are the parents of Made In Chelsea star Miles Nazaire. His parents have also made an appearance on the show a couple of times before. Learn in detail about their life and their job here.

Tag along while this article serves as a window to peek into their personal and professional lives.

Who Are MIC Miles Nazaire Parents?

Made In Chelsea‘s Miles Nazaire was born to his parents Victoria Nazaire and Jacques Nazaire. His date of birth is 12 December 1995 which makes him 26 years old as of 2022. Victoria and Jacques raised their children between Notting Hill and France.

Miles graduated from the Brit School in 2014 with a degree in Broadcast Digital Communications. Along with starting his own music label, NuffSed, and working as a filmmaker at M.N. Pictures, he has experience in many different facets of the entertainment industry. He rose to fame after getting cast on Season 15 of Made in Chelsea.

Meet MIC Miles Nazaire Mum, Victoria Nazaire

Victoria Nazaire is the mother of MIC Miles Nazaire. There are only a few times that he featured his mother on his socials. In December 2018, he posted, “With mummy acting like kids as always!”.

Miles is also featured on his mother’s Instagram. She writes, “What a wonderful flying visit to my son @milesjnazaire yesterday”. In July 2018, she made another post and wrote, “My boy came to visit #family #love #sun #togetherness #myboy #motherandson”.

  • Victoria Nazaire Age

Victoria Nazaire celebrates her birthday on 18 March. She should be above 47 years old as of 2022.

  • Victoria Nazaire Job

Victoria Nazaire is a sculptor by profession. She shares her work via her professional IG page (@victoriajnazairesculpture) and on her Facebook page. According to her Facebook, she attended MidKent College.

  • What Is Victoria Nazaire Ethnicity?

Victoria Nazaire is White-European by ethnicity.

Meet MIC Miles Nazaire Dad, Jacques Nazaire

Jacques Nazaire is probably the well-remembered among the parents of MIC Miles Nazaire. He had appeared in season 19 episode 2. Jacques announced on his Facebook, “I will be joining my son Miles Nazaire on his TV show Made in Chelsea this Monday 30 March on e4 at 9 pm”.

At Knightsbridge’s Nolita Social, Jacques gave a brief guitar jam session. Following that, Jacques got to know a few of Miles’ MIC friends. With Jacques’s presence in the E4 reality series, the audience was largely impressed.

“Miles’ dad is possibly the suavest person I’ve ever laid eyes on,” one spectator tweeted. Another said they were “Obsessed with Miles’ Dad” in their comment.

In late April 2021, Jacques posted on his Facebook, “It’s always a pleasure to be with my son Miles Nazaire in his reality tv world”. He announced reappearing in episode 4 episode which aired on 3 May 2021.

Miles is very close to his dad as well. On Father’s Day 2019 he posted a picture of his father and wrote, “Thinking of my family today! C’est mon papa”.

  • Jacques Nazaire Age

Jacques Nazaire’s age should be above 52 years old as of 2022.

  • Jacques Nazaire Job

Jacques Nazaire is a man who wears many hats. He is a musician who composes, paints, and is also a teacher.

Jacques has a LinkedIn profile that states that Artpeggios Limited is Director and Creative Director from September 2011 to December 2015. He started his career as a freelance musician in 2016.

Speaking about himself, Jacques wrote on his LinkedIn bio, “Experienced Artist with a demonstrated history of working in the music industry. Skilled in Pro Tools, Photography, Theatre, Art, and Art Direction. Strong arts and design professional with a BMus, MMus focused in Music, Electroacoustic Composition Studio Path from Goldsmiths, University of London.”

On his Facebook, Jacques stated that he formerly served as the artistic director/co-founder at Artpeggios. He founded the school in 2011 with Guillaume Bonpun, a keen amateur bassist and successful City financier.

You can learn more about his work on his website here where you can find his paintings and music.

Talking about his aspiration to be a musician, Jacques writes in his bio, “Just three years later in Bangkok as I was about to graduate from school, I met Terry Thaddeus and Noel Holmes respectively guitarist and drummer of The Hunters – a fantastic rock group from Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia. After watching them play, rehearse, jam, and go about their lives as professional musicians I decided that that was the life I wanted for me. Even though I could see how hard and unpredictable this kind of life was. But beyond any considerations, I could see it as a way for me to freely channel and explore my creativity.”

Jacques states that he practiced playing guitar 10 hours a day. He became self-taught in both music and guitar. His first professional band was Cabo Verde. He joined Emmanuel Lima, Sallah Anne, and Luis Da Silva for a residency in Agadir in Morocco.

About his academic qualifications, Jacques graduated from Goldsmiths University of London with a BMus in 2001 and with MMus in 2002.

  • What Is Jacques Nazaire Ethnicity?

Jacques Nazaire’s mother is of Indian origins (Pondichery) and a father from the French Caribbean (Guadeloupe). It is unknown what his parents worked, although as a child, Jacques traveled much. Jacques attended a boarding school in the Paris area throughout his youth and adolescence, although his last years of high school were spent in Bangkok, Thailand.
