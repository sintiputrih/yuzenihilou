What is Joel Embiid's Net Worth in 2024?

March 2024 · 3 minute read

Joel Embiid, the Cameroonian professional basketball player who currently plays as a dominant center for the Philadelphia 76ers of the NBA, has gained immense popularity and financial success throughout his career. As of March 2024, his estimated net worth stands at a staggering $100 million. This net worth is attributed to various sources such as his NBA salary, endorsement deals, and investments, solidifying his status as one of the highest-paid athletes in the league.

Early Life and NBA Career

Birth and Draft

Joel Hans Embiid was born on March 16, 1994, in Yaoundé, Cameroon. He ventured into the NBA after playing college basketball at Kansas Jayhawks for a year and was subsequently drafted by the Philadelphia 76ers as the third overall pick during the first round of the 2014 NBA draft.

Career Achievements

Despite facing early-career challenges due to injuries, Embiid has demonstrated exceptional talent on the court, earning several prestigious awards such as All-NBA Second Team and All-Defensive Second Team, as well as being a six-time All-Star since 2018.

Sources of Income

NBA Salary

Embiid's current contract with the Philadelphia 76ers fetches him an impressive salary of $53,320,232 million per year, which is expected to increase annually. This recent four-year super-max extension signed by Embiid is worth a remarkable $196 million.

Endorsements

Embiid has also amassed significant income through lucrative endorsement deals. Notably, his partnership with Under Armour, which signed him to a five-year deal worth up to $80 million back in 2018, has notably contributed to his net worth. Additionally, he has collaborated with other renowned brands such as Crypto.com, PepsiCo, Mountain Dew, EA, and Amazon, earning millions annually.

Investments

Recognizing the importance of financial prudence, Embiid has diversified his income through wise investments in stocks, real estate properties, and ownership stakes in esports teams and popular sports apparel companies, as highlighted in Forbes magazine.

Personal Life and Philanthropy

Marital Status

Contrary to rumors, Joel Embiid is not single; he married Brazilian model Anne De Paula in July 2023, and the couple welcomed a son together.

Charitable Contributions

Embiid is also known for his philanthropic endeavors, having donated $500k to the coronavirus relief fund in 2020. His commitment to serving those in need demonstrates his generosity beyond the realm of professional basketball.

Joel Embiid's journey from Cameroon to becoming one of the highest-earning and most accomplished athletes in the NBA is a testament to his unwavering dedication and talent. His net worth of $100 million in 2024 reflects his exceptional success on the court, lucrative endorsement deals, and astute investment decisions. Moreover, his commitment to philanthropy and family values adds depth to his inspiring story, solidifying his status as a role model for aspiring athletes worldwide.
