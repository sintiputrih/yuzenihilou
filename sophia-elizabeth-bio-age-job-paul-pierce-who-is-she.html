Paul and Julie began dating in 2005, while Paul was still playing for the Boston Celtics. The pair then got married in July 2010 at a red-themed wedding ceremony at the Resort at Pelican Hill in Newport Coast, California.

Paul’s wife, Julie Pierce (nee Landrum) is a philanthropist as well as an interior designer. She has a degree in fashion buying and merchandising from the Fashion Institute of Technology. She studied interior design at the New York School of Interior Design. Julie has her own business, through which she designs houses across the United States and Canada.
