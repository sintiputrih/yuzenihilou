How To Heal In Baldur's Gate 3

September 2024 · 2 minute read

Healing party members in Baldur’s Gate 3 will ensure that your party is always ready for a fight and they’ll last longer. Keeping everyone’s health in check can be a bit cumbersome and this guide will show you how to heal party members in Baldur’s Gate 3.

How To Heal In Baldur’s Gate 3

As you might have noticed, the scope of Baldur’s Gate 3 is massive and there are a lot of things that you can do and accomplish in this amazing game but one of the most primary functions of healing has a few methods that you can try out.

There are multiple ways in which you can heal in Baldur’s Gate 3, depending on the need of healing that you need you can approach different options in the game. Make sure that your party members are all well and healthy so that there’s a minimal hindrance to your objectives and missions in Baldur’s Gate 3.

The most basic thing that you can do is see if you’re well-stocked with food, food can help heal yourself and your party members in Baldur’s Gate 3. This is really an inexpensive way to heal and depending on the item the healing rate will vary.

Apples and well-cooked meals offer a great way to regain lost health and replenish yourself before continuing on your journey. Use this when you’re at a lower level to quickly bring someone up to speed with the rest of your squad.

Another way to heal in Baldur’s Gate 3 is by using a short break by setting up a camp that will work wonders if you can find the time to rest your party. You can only use this once in a day so make sure that you time your rests well in Baldur’s Gate 3.

Other than that, there are a variety of healing potions that you can purchase from merchants across the lands, you can also get them by looting boxes, backpacks, dead bodies and as rewards for completing tasks in the game.

If you have a spellcaster in your party, you can even use spells to heal yourself and your party in Baldur’s Gate 3 but the regeneration of spells takes a while so use them wisely in the game.

This is all there is to know about how to heal in Baldur’s Gate 3, make sure to check out how to dismiss party members in Baldur’s Gate, you can add new characters by letting go of the current ones to balance out your party in the game.
