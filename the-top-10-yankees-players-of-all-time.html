The Top 10 Yankees Players of All Time

June 2024 Â· 4 minute read

When it comes to the history of baseball, few teams have left as big of an impact as the New York Yankees. With a rich history spanning over a century, the Yankees have boasted some of the greatest players to ever step foot on a baseball field. From the legendary Babe Ruth to the iconic Derek Jeter, here are the top 10 Yankees players of all time.

1. Babe Ruth

No list of the greatest Yankees players would be complete without mentioning Babe Ruth. Known as the Sultan of Swat, Ruth revolutionized the game with his powerful swing and incredible home run record. His larger-than-life personality and unmatched talent made him a legend both on and off the field. With seven World Series titles and a career batting average of .342, Ruth is undoubtedly the greatest Yankee of all time.

2. Lou Gehrig

Nicknamed the Iron Horse, Lou Gehrig was a dominant force during his time with the Yankees. With his incredible durability and consistent performance, Gehrig set numerous records, including the most consecutive games played. A true team player and leader, Gehrig's career was sadly cut short by a devastating illness, but his impact on the game will never be forgotten.

3. Joe DiMaggio

Known as the Yankee Clipper, Joe DiMaggio was a true superstar during his career. With his graceful playing style and remarkable hitting ability, DiMaggio captured the hearts of fans across the nation. He holds the record for the longest hitting streak in baseball history, with 56 consecutive games. DiMaggio's excellence on the field, combined with his iconic marriage to Marilyn Monroe, cemented his status as one of the greatest Yankees of all time.

4. Mickey Mantle

Mickey Mantle, also known as the Mick, was a true powerhouse for the Yankees. With his incredible speed and raw power, Mantle was a force to be reckoned with. He won three MVP awards and helped lead the Yankees to numerous World Series victories. Despite battling injuries throughout his career, Mantle's impact on the game cannot be overstated.

5. Derek Jeter

Derek Jeter is not only one of the greatest Yankees of all time, but also one of the most beloved players in baseball history. With his clutch performances and exceptional leadership, Jeter was the face of the Yankees for over two decades. He holds numerous records, including the most hits in Yankees history. Jeter's professionalism and dedication to the game made him a true icon.

6. Yogi Berra

Yogi Berra is not only known for his quirky sayings, but also for his incredible talent on the baseball field. As a catcher, Berra was a key player in the Yankees' success during the 1950s and 1960s. He won three MVP awards and was a part of an astonishing 10 World Series championship teams. Berra's ability to handle pitchers and his clutch hitting made him one of the greatest catchers of all time.

7. Mariano Rivera

When it comes to closers, no one has been as dominant as Mariano Rivera. With his devastating cut fastball, Rivera was virtually unhittable in the late innings. He holds the record for the most saves in MLB history and was a key component of the Yankees' dynasty in the late 1990s and early 2000s. Rivera's calm demeanor and ability to perform under pressure made him one of the greatest relief pitchers of all time.

8. Whitey Ford

Whitey Ford, also known as the Chairman of the Board, was a pitching legend for the Yankees. With his impeccable control and ability to outsmart hitters, Ford won numerous games and helped lead the Yankees to multiple championships. He holds the record for the most wins in Yankees history and was a key member of the team's success during the 1950s and 1960s.

9. Reggie Jackson

Reggie Jackson, also known as Mr. October, was a true superstar for the Yankees. With his incredible power and clutch hitting, Jackson was a force to be reckoned with during the playoffs. He hit three home runs in a single World Series game and helped lead the Yankees to two championships. Jackson's charisma and ability to deliver in the biggest moments made him a fan favorite.

10. Don Mattingly

Don Mattingly, also known as Donnie Baseball, was a fan favorite during his time with the Yankees. With his smooth swing and exceptional defense, Mattingly was one of the most complete players of his era. He won three Gold Glove awards and was a perennial All-Star. Although Mattingly's career was plagued by injuries, his impact on the game cannot be overlooked.

The New York Yankees have been blessed with some of the greatest players in baseball history. From Babe Ruth to Derek Jeter, these players have left an indelible mark on the game and the Yankees franchise. Their talent, leadership, and dedication to the game have made them true legends and will forever be remembered as the top 10 Yankees players of all time.
