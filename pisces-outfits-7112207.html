13 Pisces Outfits to Wear, According to an Astrologer

February 2024 · 3 minute read

Known for their tender hearts, intuition, and imagination, Pisces tend to choose clothes that speak to the sign's dreamy spirit. In other words, great Pisces outfits consist of bohemian styles that show off their soulful nature, such as flowy dresses and floral prints, but at the same time, include items that are playful and bold, leaning into aesthetics like balletcore and Barbiecore (Barbie herself is a Pisces, according to her birthdate).

The transcendent fish doesn't shy away from lush fabrics in every color variation (think boho icons Erykah Badu and Drew Barrymore), but when they're trying to dress more low-key, a Breton striped top and jeans will do the trick. Olivia Wilde (who is a Pisces) is a fan of this classic combo, while fellow Pisces queen Rihanna also serves up plenty of outfit inspiration for those born between Feb. 19 and March 10, stepping out in vibrant colors and magical prints.

If you're hoping to come up with something fun to wear that speaks to your true self, here are a few Pisces outfit ideas, ahead.

Fish-Themed

The most literal way to dress like a Pisces is to bring in some fish designs—it is their literal sign, after all—and have that be the statement piece in a look. The gloves and boots help the outfit from being too "twee" or on the nose.

Statement-Making Stripes

On the opposite side of the Pisces coin is the dresser who loves to make a statement. They're not going all-out in head-to-toe red, but they are incorporating elements like stripes and graphic pieces into their looks. Also: fish bag!

Whimsical Prints

Who can pull off green sequin boots? The Pisces can! Frankly, by treating it as a neutral (you can easily see this outfit working with black or white boots) they bring in just a dash of their funky weirdness, but not so much that it ruins the outfit.

A Flowy Dress

Flowy dresses are key for Pisces. After all, they need something to wear that gives them flexibility in movement. 

Balletcore 

A ballet wrap top or tulle skirt will remind Pisces of their delicate and beautiful soul, as well as their romantic and artistic sentiments.

A Vegan Leather Blazer

A boho-inspired leather blazer will keep Pierces warm all winter long (especially if they add plenty of layers to the look).

Daytime Sequins

Pisces are very glamorous. They like to dress up whenever given the chance, which is why a sequin bodysuit, skirt, or shiny pants make for an ideal daytime outfit, too.

Florals 

The beauty is in the details for Pisces, who tend to overlook them. All the more reason for those under this sign to embrace the softness and triviality of spring floral prints.

Eyelet Designs 

Pisces have innocence to them and eyelet fabric captures that same sweet vibe. An eyelet dress or skirt is the perfect Pisces outfit when this sign is looking for some simplicity in their lives.

Crochet Items 

Crochet pieces make up the perfect Pisces outfit to wear when relaxing at the beach. They’ll fall in love with the net-like nature — which is on-theme all around!

Bright Pants 

Pants in a cool shade of purple (or any color) will brighten up their days. If you're hoping for some positive vibes, pull on a bold pair.

A Breezy Skirt 

A silk or satin slip skirt is a reference to the mermaid tail that Pisces wish they had in real life. It represents this sign's spiritual energy.

Barbiecore 

Barbie is a Pisces, so go ahead and lean into the vibe! Embrace your fantasies — the more elegant, extravagant, and pinker, the better!
