Donny Davis Cause Of Death

Donny David, a Las Vegas impersonator/comedian, has died. Because an autopsy has yet to be carried out, his cause of death is not publicly known as of 23rd Feb 2022.

According to the report of TMZ, the comedian was found dead inside a room at the Resorts World Hotel. The outlet reported that he “may have died as a result of foul play” based on the police report that the news outlet obtained. According to Las Vegas Police, he reportedly hung out at Dawg House bar around 1 a.m. with another man and two women.

Later on, the group headed to get drinks at the Flamingo, before returning to his hotel at Resort World around 2:30 a.m. According to TMZ, one of the women in the group said that Donny was denied alcohol at their final destination as he was “too intoxicated”. Still, Donny and his guy friend were said to have gone back to the comedian’s room at 3:30 a.m. where they stayed for almost an hour.

When they returned at 5 in the morning, they noticed Donny’s hand looking pale and turning purple as he sat in a chair. One of his female friends claimed Donny’s had was ‘freezing’ and he was no longer breathing which made them call security who called the police.

Donny was declared dead at 7 a.m. at Sunrise Hospital.

As for the “foul play,” police report revealed that Donny’s friend refused to speak to them without a lawyer. Police also had trouble finding people who lived in the room with Donny.

Obituary started pouring in for the comedian.

Donny’s friend Jeff Beacher told DailyMail, “Today I didn’t just lose a friend. I lost a dream, a brother, a best friend. Donny was not just a loyal loving best friend. I trusted him with everything from my life to my business and everything in between. Donny was an Angel. ”

Meanwhile, Jeff also admired his friendship with the late comedian while speaking with the outlet. He then ended by saying, “The planet was a better place with you on it. I will continue our dream and win awards for our documentary and continue to push equality for everyone in your honor. I love you, Donny. You’re the best.”

Along with Jeff, Ryan Phillippe, comedian Jeffrey Ross, Nicky Hilton, and Paris Hilton also paid their tribute.

Donny Davis Wife

Donny Davis was likely unmarried at the time of his death. So, he probably didn’t have a wife when he passed away. Back in March 2016, he had posted a tribute to his friend Lil Kim (@thereallilkim) who had died suddenly. According to his post, he previously dated her for quite some time.

Whereas in 2013, Donny posted a picture of himself with a beautiful woman on Facebook. He claimed her to be his future wife in the comment section of his FB post. Other than that, he never discussed his relationship publicly on social media platforms.

Donny Davis Career

Donny Davis is a graduate of Richland High School. He graduated in 1996 then attended the University of Washington and graduated in 2000. He was the head little person/Performer / Actor / Production at BEACHER’S MADHOUSE. 

Donny had found success as an impersonator and comedian. The famed impersonator joined Spears’ residency in Las Vegas as a dancer in 2013. Hence, he often referred to singer Britney Spears as his boss on his social media posts. After discovering Donny online, nightlife entrepreneur Jeff Beacher hired Davis to perform at the Beacher’s Madhouse nightclub in Los Angeles and later, when the show relocated, at the MGM Grand Las Vegas.

Moreover, his thriving career provided him with an opportunity to work with the likes of Seth McFarland, Mariah Carey, Joe Jonas, Kelly Osbourne, and Hayden Panettiere. Deadline reported, that he also made appearances in the 2017 comedy It’s Gawd! and 2013’s Pain & Gain.

Donny had become a Las Vegas icon with the routine he performed at the Beacher’s Madhouse and other Las Vegas gems. His fans loved him for his performance as “mini-Trump” impressions along with many others.

Before his death, Donny was reportedly working on a podcast series with Kelly Osbourne and Jeff Beacher. He also made frequent appearances on Chelsea Lately.
