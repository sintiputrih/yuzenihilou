Kendall Jenner's Must-Have Chokers Are All $49 or Above

July 2024 · 2 minute read

Getting collared! Kendall Jenner recently shared that she styles nearly all of her outfits with black chokers.

“These days, it’s rare that I’m not wearing a choker, lol,” she said in a new post on her website, kendallj.com, on Friday, July 1. “I love how simple they are and how they can make any outfit trendy — even just jeans and a tee.”

But the Keeping Up With the Kardashians star’s go-to versions cost more than the typical trendy necklace. Jenner, 20, is a fan of the L.A.-based brand Are You Am I, and she put together a list of the jewelry maker’s must-have items. The roundup includes a range of skinny leather wrap necklaces and thick velvet options starting at $49.

Are You Am I Double Wrap Lilou Choker, $59, areyouami.com

Are You Am I Fina Choker, $49, areyouami.com

Are You Am I Ambrosia Velvet Choker, $89, areyouami.com

Are You Am I Rosalie Velvet Choker, $59, areyouami.com

If an $50-plus choker isn’t in your price range, have no fear. The Estée Lauder brand ambassador is also fond of DIY-ing, and even created a how-to video about making the trendy necklace on your own on her site. All you need: velvet ribbon, safety pins, fusible bonding web and an iron. “My stylist Monica Rose makes these exact chokers for my everyday accessories!” Jenner explained in the clip.
