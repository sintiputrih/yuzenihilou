Chrisean Rock Streams Live On IG While In Labor, Video Sparks Debate Online

June 2024 · 5 minute read

Rapper and reality TV personality Chrisean Rock welcomed her baby with Blueface into the world on Sunday, September 3, 2023. The rapper took the unique step of live-streaming her delivery on her official Instagram account, where she was surrounded by her family.

At one point during the broadcast, she even shouted for assistance in delivering her baby.

The exact duration of Chrisean Rock‘s labor remains unconfirmed, but the live stream captured the sheer joy and relief in her tears following the birth of her baby boy.

In a heartfelt tribute to her new arrival, Rock named the baby Chrisean Malone, a namesake that pays homage to her own.

Before the delivery, the rapper had taken to Instagram to alert her followers that she was in labor, with footage showing medical professionals attending to her.

In another video, she mentioned that her water had broken a few days earlier but described it as a “slow leak,” which left her feeling somewhat perplexed.

The week before, in a “last pregnancy (photo) dump before (her) baby boy comes,” the Rainy Days singer had revealed her baby’s gender. Among the images shared was a 3D ultrasound photo displaying the baby’s facial features, along with a glimpse of her baby shower.

The live-stream event garnered significant attention from netizens, with over 315,000 viewers tuning in to share their thoughts.

While many offered their congratulations and appreciated Chrisean’s decision not to immediately reveal her baby’s face, others criticized her for live-streaming her delivery, particularly her use of profanities during labor.

In contrast, some fans recognize that childbirth can be an incredibly painful experience, and it’s not uncommon for mothers to vocalize their pain through screams.
