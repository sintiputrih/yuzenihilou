Jason Bolden’s Wiki – He is a Style Guru For The Red Carpet In Hollywood

Moving from St. Louis to Chicago with $500 and dream of being a doctor, the story of Jason Bolden is itself as a movie.

A native of St. Louis, Missouri, Jason was born on February 27, 1982. Spending his childhood with memories of skating, he was supposed to work in the medical field.

Apparently, fashion was not a thing in mid-west back in the days. Therefore, completing his school, he moved to Chicago, Illinois.

He then attended Northwestern University for pre-med studies. During the time, he had some economic crisis on hand.

As a result, he started working at Cynthia Rowley. In fact, he was so devoted to fashion during the time he decided to change the path in his career.

Eventually, he left the pre-med classes and transferred to the Art Institute of Chicago. Motivated by his grandmother, he has tasted every pinch of success till the date.

His journey started with the AGT judge and American actress ‘Gabrielle Union‘. After working for a number of retails, he relocated to New York and inaugurated his new clothing store ‘The Garment Room‘.

Jason commenced his celebrity design with Dwayne Wade’s spouse Gabrielle. Later, he went to design for famous names to extend his portfolio.

Likewise, he managed the wardrobe for Taraji P. Henson, Yara Shahidi, Sareena Williams, Sabrina Carpenter, and Storm Rein.

Apparently, the list goes on with famous names like Wiz Khalifa, Sasha Lane, Tika Sumpter and many more.
