20 Great Mothers Day Gift Ideas

March 2024 · 9 minute read

Our 2024 Mother's Day gift guide includes thoughtful gift ideas for every budget! Find gorgeous cookbooks, tasty treats, handy gadgets, and more.

Mother’s Day is right around the corner! If you need gift ideas—especially for a mom who likes to cook—you’re in the right place.

Below, I’ve rounded up my picks for the best Mother’s Day gifts. They include thoughtful presents for every budget—creative cookbooks, all-star appliances, fun gadgets, treats, and more. One could be the perfect present for a maternal figure in your life.

Have fun checking them out, and to any moms reading, happy (early) Mother’s Day!

Our Best Mother’s Day Gift Ideas

1. Love & Lemons Simple Feel Good Food

Shameless plug: My latest cookbook, Love & Lemons Simple Feel Good Food, would be a great gift for Mother’s Day!

This book is designed to help readers get a delicious, plant-forward meal on the table without a lot of fuss.

Half the recipes are “at-the-ready recipes,” which are easy to make with basic ingredients. The other half are “in advance recipes,” which are designed to prep ahead.

This book is PACKED with fun features like

  • 3-in-1 meal plans – Each plan consists of 3 dinner recipes that share a grocery list. Components from one day flow into the next to reduce waste and simplify prep!
  • Love & Lemons signature graphics and charts – Like a Guide to Lasagna Layers, Mix & Match Tahini Cookies, Rainbow Blender Soups, and more

Moms looking for fresh, flavorful meal inspiration will love this book!

$19.69 at Amazon$19.69 at Target

2. Love & Lemons Every Day

My second cookbook, Love & Lemons Every Day, is a lovely Mother’s Day gift as well.

It features 100+ delicious, veggie-packed recipes for every occasion, from quick weekday breakfasts to holiday dinners.

You’ll find fun graphic charts and cooking resources in this book too, like

  • A Giant Grid of Salad Dressings, featuring 16 5-ingredient dressings
  • Roasting Vegetables from A to Z, which will teach you how to roast 30+ veggies!
  • 6 Kinds of Date Balls, aka the best healthy snacks

Deciding between my second book and my latest? Simple Feel Good Food is focused on regular breakfasts, lunches, and especially dinners. Love & Lemons Every Day includes a mix of weekday dishes and special occasion recipes, plus extras like sides, cocktails, and snacks!

$19.79 at Amazon$19.79 at Target

3. The Love & Lemons Cookbook

Does your mom love shopping at the farmers market? Get her my first cookbook—The Love & Lemons Cookbook—as a Mother’s Day gift.

Organized by vegetable from A to Z, it’s full of simple, vibrant recipes to help readers showcase their farmers market finds.

$23.99 at Amazon

4. Plant Magic

I got a sneak peek at Plant Magic a few months ago, and I fell head over heels for it.

Yes, this cookbook is bursting with delicious, creative plant-based recipes. But that’s not all! My favorite thing about it is author Desiree Nielsen’s writing. A registered dietician, she shares essays and thoughts on living and eating well, empowering you to choose foods (any foods!) and activities that make you feel good.

It’d be a wonderful present for a mom who loves reading and reflection as much as a good meal.

$25.00 at Amazon

5. Around Our Table

You might know Sara Forte from her blog and meal planning club Sprouted Kitchen. If not, you should! She writes whole foods, produce-forward recipes that are inventive, elegant, and simple enough to fit into everyday life.

Her new cookbook Around Our Table comes out on April 30, but you can preorder it now—just in time for Mother’s Day! I got an early copy a few weeks ago, and I think it would be a perfect gift. It’s filled with mouthwatering recipes (heads up: not all are vegetarian) as well as musings on motherhood that will resonate with many moms and caretakers.

$40.00 at Amazon

6. Ippodo Matcha

When I tested and reviewed the best matcha powders last year, the Ippodo Tea Ummon was my favorite by far. With its vibrant green color and rich, earthy flavor, it’s excellent in traditional matcha tea and lattes. If a mom you know is a matcha drinker, she’ll love this high-quality powder.

7. Matcha Whisk Set

This matcha whisk set is another of the best Mother’s Day gift ideas for matcha-drinking moms!

This affordable set includes a bamboo whisk, wooden scoop, matcha spoon, and ceramic whisk holder. The traditional design of the whisk, or chasen, makes it easy to mix up frothy, lump-free matcha tea. Cleanup is also a breeze—just wash the whisk and place it on the ceramic stand to dry!

$12.59 at Amazon

8. Electric Tea Kettle

Know a mom who can’t start her day without a cup of coffee or tea? Consider this Bodum electric kettle as a Mother’s Day gift.

It quickly and efficiently boils water (and automatically shuts itself off). The gooseneck spout gives you control over the speed and consistency of the water flow as you pour, making it ideal for pour over fans. The stainless steel body and cork handle are lightweight and easy to maneuver too.

$32.99 at Amazon

Staub Dutch ovens aren’t cheap Mother’s Day gifts, but these high-quality, versatile pots will last a lifetime.

I use mine daily for making soups, sauces, pastas, braises, bread, and more! The enameled cast-iron material conducts heat evenly and efficiently and is easy to clean with just soap and water. I love that it can transition seamlessly from the stovetop to the oven too.

The colorful pots also look gorgeous, making them perfect for serving food at the table. The new citron option is SO bright and festive for spring!
