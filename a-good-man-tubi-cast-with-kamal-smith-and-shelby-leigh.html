A Good Man Tubi Cast With Kamal Smith and Shelby Leigh

May 2024 · 5 minute read

A Good Man Tubi cast consists of Kamal Smith and Ebony Tates in lead roles. The crime thriller will be released on April 27, 2023.

The show focuses on businessman Ethan (Kamal Smith), who can do everything for the love of his life. He divorced his ex-wife after six years of marriage life after he discovers the affair.

He is searching for a new relationship after his separation.

After his business partner Matt’s (Robert Q. Jackson) advice, he starts dating a new girl Arianna (Ebony Tates).

However, she turns out to be the wrong choice as she shows familiar signs.

The show was written and directed by Joe Smith. Shelby is the first assistant director and one of the supporting cast in the movie.

The show premiered at the Riviera Cinema, 30170 Grand River Ave, Farmington Hills, MI, on February 10, 2023.

Meet The Cast Of A Good Man On Tubi

A Good Man Tubi cast includes Kamal Smith and Ebony Tates. Shelby Leigh and King Wesley are the supporting characters.

Kamal Smith As Ethan Carter

Smith portrays the role of the lead character Ethan Carter. His wife cheats on him, and he starts looking for a new relationship to get over her.

The couple split after six years of married life.

He tries to go to a therapist, but his business partner suggests that men like him don’t do therapy.

He advises him to start drinking and find a new partner. As a result, he meets Arianna at a Hibachi Go. Her card gets declined, and he pays for her.

The businessman starts dating her and even proposes to her on the boat.

He starts suspecting his fiancee is doing something, but he still buys and does things for her.

The Detroit-born actor Kamal Smith debuted in the 2008 movie as Lionel. He is known for his work in He Played Me, Coffee Talk, and The Wrong One.

He directed and wrote Loud Pack, Cofee Talk, Fireworks Fireworks, and A Week Ago, and He played Me.

The upcoming films Smokin’ Good and The Wrong One are also written by him. He starred in others movies like Sincerely and Captured.

Ebony Tates As Arianna

Tates stars as Arianna, who starts dating Ethan. She met him while she was at a Hibachi Go.

When her card gets declined, he helps her out. They eventually start dating.

She previously dated Chaos, who almost killed her in a drive-by shooting.

Although she is unsure about loving him, she gets engaged to him. Things turn out wild when she cheats on her fiancee.

The American actress began her career with stage performance. She debuted in the TV series Carter and Cody as Micah in 2015.

She played on Stay in Your Place, DaGram, Birthday Behaviour, The Mule, My Mom Made Me Do It, and Deadly Secrets.

She will be in the pre-production movie Hurt People as Nessa.

King Wesley as Chaos

Wesley appeared as Chaos, the ex-partner of Arianna.

When he gets out of jail, he makes contact with his ex-lover Ari, despite her being engaged with a new partner.

He also has a son from his fiancee. He works for Ethan renovating the house for Ari.

The Yonkers-born actor showed love for acting in 2009 when he started performing in a stage play.

He is an actor and producer acknowledged for his appearance in Escape Plan: The Extractors, Cocde of Thieves, Ear 2 Ear, and Flint Tale.

He debuted in Carter and Cody as Phillip in 2015. He starred in Tantalum, Detroiters, True Law, The Bobby Brown Story, and Candy.

He will star in the upcoming video Street Numbers & Street Sweepers and Sasha Lanes.
