Vanessa Lachey- Birth, Age, Ethnicity, Siblings, Parents

Vanessa Lachey was born in Angeles, Pampanga, Philippines on November 9, 1980. Vince Minnillo and Helen Berecro are her parents. Her father is an American citizen of Italian and Irish origin who works in the Air Force and lives in Cleveland, Ohio. Her mother was born in Manila, Philippines. She used to go by the name Vanessa Joy Minnillo before she married. Vincent Minnillo, her two-year-old adopted brother, was her childhood companion. Her parents divorced in 1986 after splitting up in 1983. Both of them remarried. As a result, Vanessa and her brother, together with their mother and stepfather, moved to Turkey. Later, in 1991, they relocated permanently to Charleston, South Carolina, to live with their father and stepmother Donna.

Because of her father’s career in the Air Force, the family used to move around a lot. As a result, she was never able to commit to a single school. While living in Washington, California, Nevada, Florida, Germany, and Japan for nine years, she attended eight different schools. She attended the Roman Catholic Bishop England High School after returning to her father’s home and eventually settling down. She used to be a high school cheerleader. For a year, she also attended the Assumption Catholic School in San Bernardino, California.
