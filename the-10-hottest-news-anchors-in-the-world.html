10. Julie Banderas

Julie Banderas, originally named Julie E. Bidwell was born on 25th September 1973 and is one of the sizzling hot news anchors in the world.

She is an American news anchor for the Fox News Channel, which is based in New York City.

Likewise, she works as a co-host at “Fox and Friends First” for the Fox News Channel.

Born in Hartford, U.S., she started her career at WLVI-TV in Massachusetts.

After several years of hard work at different television networks, she joined Fox News Channel as a General Assignment Reporter in 2005.

Julie Banderas came in highlight in 2006 after the on-air clash with spoke person of the Baptist Church (former anchor) Shirley Phelps-Roper.

Afterward, she also hosted for Fox Report Weekend but moved to a weekly anchor role.

Meanwhile, you can see her in America’s News Headquarters or Fox Report Weekend.

So stay tuned with the news if you want to catch a glimpse of this beauty!!

9. Molly Line

Another hottest news anchor Molly Line was born on 31st January 1977. She is an American reporter and anchor.

Initially, she was a Boston-based correspondent for Fox News Channel in 2006.

In the meantime, she is a news correspondent for Fox News Channel in New York.

Similarly, Molly Line has a fascinating story. She not only is one of the hottest news anchors but also a winner of the Miss Greater Bluefield Area Pageant.

Likewise, she competed for Miss West Virginia Beauty Pageant in 1997 and 2000 after graduating from high school.

However, Molly chose the news reporting career and pursued a degree in Mass Communication and Political Science.

After that, she began her reporting career at WDTV-TV (CBS 5) as a photographer and reporter.

Before joining Fox News as a Correspondent, she worked as an anchor and reporter for WFXT-TV (FOX 25) and WXXA-TV (FOX 23).

Nonetheless, she is one of the successful and hottest news anchors globally.

8. Betty Nguyen

Another one of the hottest news anchors is Betty Nguyen, who was born on 1st September 1974. Betty Nguyen is a Vietnamese-American news anchor.

She served as a news anchor in different network channels such as CNN, NBC News, CBS News, and MSNBC.

For the time being, she is currently working at WPIX, which is headquartered in New York City. Betty is a news anchor for the PIX11 Morning News.

She has a mixed ancestry as she is of Vietnamese, American, and Scottish descent.

Similarly, she has covered various significant stories and has also been awarded for her audacity.

Betty won a Peabody Award for her coverage of the tsunami in South Asia and Hurricane Katrina.

In 2007, the Smithsonian Institute honored her as the first Vietnamese-American anchor in a national television news program in the United States.

Furthermore, she was inducted into the Asian Hall of Fame in 2015 for her incredible work.

Therefore, Betty Nguyen is not only a hottie but also a pretty affluent news anchor.

7. Ghida Fakhry

Ghida Fakhry was born on 1st January 1970 and is one of the hottest news anchors in the world. She is a Lebanese-British reporter and news anchor.

Initially, she started her career as a CNN world report contributor and a Middle East analyst.

Likewise, Ghida Frakhry worked as lead anchor for the Al Jazeera news channel and as a primary anchor in Doha.

Similarly, in 2010, Ghida Fakhry hosted an award-winning documentary program “Witness.”

She has a reputation as a fantastic interviewer as she conducted interviews with many U.S. officials, senior members of the State Department, and Pentagon officials in Washington D.C.

Moreover, she covered the 9/11 attacks in 2000 as a New York Correspondent and Bureau Chief for Al Jazeera.

Ghida Fakhry has won several awards for her notable works.

She was voted as one of four US-based news anchors in Esquire Magazine’s annual ‘Women We Love’ in 2007.

Furthermore, in 2012, she was honored with the George Foster Peabody Award for Al Jazeera’s coverage of the Arab Awakening.

Additionally, she was also named along with the World’s Most Influential Arabs by Arabian Business Power 500 in 2013.

Well, Ghida is not only a beauty but a woman with incredible determination.

Liz Cho is an American news anchor and journalist.

She started her professional career as an assignment editor at New England Cable News in Massachusetts.

As of now, she has been a news anchor at WABC-TV based in New York City since 2003.

She co-anchored “Eyewitness News” with David Novarro at four and Bill Ritter at 6 p.m.

Liz Cho is of mixed descent, Korean from her father’s side and Jewish from her mother’s. No wonder why she is such a beauty!!

Moreover, she was listed as one of the “50 Most Beautiful People” by People Magazine in 2003.

She has a daughter named Louisa Simone Gottlieb born in 2007. Although she got divorced, she is a wonderful mother to her child.

Furthermore, Liz is not only an affluent news anchor but also a great human being.

3. Robin Meade

The third hottest anchor in the world is Robin Michelle Meade, born on 21st, April 1969. She is an American author and news anchor.

Robin Meade initiated her broadcasting career with local stations in Ohio. Likewise, she joined an American cable news channel (HLN) in 2001.

For the time being, she is the lead news anchor for HLN’s morning show “Morning Express.”

She was a winner of Miss Ohio in 1992. Also, she has released two country music albums.

Moreover, in 1995 Robin got a regional Emmy Award for covering the 1995 Fox River Grove bus-train collision.

In 2005, she was awarded a Young Alumnus Award for her accomplishments in broadcasting by Ashland University.

Robin Meade is a perfect blend of beauty with multiple capabilities.

Georgie Thompson, original name Georgina Jane Ainslie or Lady Ainslie, born on 25th September 1977 is the second hottest anchor globally.

Likewise, she started her career after graduating as a production assistant and production scheme for GMTV.

After that, she moved to Sky Sports in 2001.

Georgie Thompson is a beautiful sports reporter and has covered many sporting events, including US Open Tennis, Speedway World Cup, 2011 Wimbledon Championships, America’s Cup, among others.

However, she left Sky Sports News in 2011 and became a presenter on Sky Sports F1.

Moreover, in 2013, she moved to Fox Sports as a panelist on the “Crowd Goes Wild.”

Georgie Thompson was voted the 93rd “Sexiest Female in the World” by the British men’s magazine FHM in 2007.

The number one hottest news anchor on the list is Melissa Theuriau born on 18th, July 1978. She is a well-known French news anchor and journalist.

Meanwhile, she works as a news anchor for M6 Television Channel.
