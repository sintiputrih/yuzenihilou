Andy Dalton family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Andrew Dalton is a professional American football quarterback who currently plays for the New Orleans Saints in the NFL.

Dalton, often known as “The Red Rifle,” has previously played for the Cincinnati Bengals, Dallas Cowboys, and Chicago Bears. He attended TCU, where he became the school’s all-time leader in quarterback wins and won the Rose Bowl in 2011

Andy Dalton wife

The quarterback has been married to his lovely wife Jordan since 2011. Dalton redshirted as a freshman in college but traveled to every road game with the team.

He was competing with Marcus Jackson for the starting quarterback position heading into the 2007 season. Coach Gary Patterson named Dalton the starter two weeks before the season opener against Baylor.

Andy Dalton children

Dalton and his wife currently have three children. Nash Gordon and Noah Andrew are the first two but the name of their third child who was born in 2019 is currently not in the public domain.

Andy Dalton parents

He was brought into this world by Greg and Tina Dalton.

Andy Dalton siblings

Dalton has two siblings Avery and Ashley Dalton.
