10 Best Drugstore Waterproof Eyeliners for Perfect Eyes All Day

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Regardless of the time of year, you want an eyeliner that is easy to apply and stays put, without running or fading by noon. In the summer, you are battling sweat, humidity, and mid-afternoon thunderstorms thrown into the mix, making it really hard to keep your eyes smudge-free. In the colder months, you are battling seasonal colds and wind, which means your eyes will water more or your may rub them.

That's why waterproof eyeliners exist. Much like a bottle of wine, water-resistant eyeliners are one makeup product that doesn't have to be expensive to be good. Whether you swear by liquid liner or a kohl pencil, there are a ton of affordable options that you can pick up during a drugstore run. The best part? All of our favorites are under $11.

Keep scrolling for the 10 best drugstore waterproof eyeliners that won't budge, no matter the weather.

Maybelline Eye Studio Lasting Drama Gel Eyeliner

When you've finally mastered the cat-eye, you want it to stay on for as long as possible (just in case nailing it was a total fluke). This gel pot is your best bet: It stays smudge-free for up to 24 hours.

L'Oréal Paris Infallible Pro-Last Waterproof Eyeliner

If you're new to wearing eyeliner, start with L'Oréal's gel pencil. The creamy formula glides on effortlessly and can be used for drawing thick or thin lines.

NYX Retractable Eyeliner

The ultra-fine tip of NYX's pencil makes it perfect for defining your waterline. Priced at a budget-friendly $5, this waterproof eyeliner comes in nine shades.

e.l.f. Cosmetics Intense H2O Proof Eyeliner Pen

Similar to how a felt-tip pen keeps handwriting from looking too messy, this eyeliner keeps lines clean and precise. It applies color smoothly without any flakes or feathering.

CoverGirl Liquiline Blast Eyeliner

Nothing says "badass" quite like intentionally smudged eyeliner. That being said, even though this dual-ended CoverGirl eyeliner has a built-in smudger, once you soften up your line you don't want it melting further down your face. Luckily, the jet-black formula is completely waterproof.

Almay Top of the Line Eyeliner

When you've spent a few extra minutes doing your eyeliner, you don't want the attention to be on your red, bloodshot eyes. Skip irritation by reaching for Almay's eyeliner. The water-resistant formula is infused with gentle ingredients like vitamin E, which nourishes and protects the eye area.

Essence Superlast Eyeliner

Thanks to the tapered tip of Essence's liquid eyeliner, you can easily apply a cat-eye in a single stroke. For a more dramatic finish, use the brush to trace the shape of your wing, then go back and fill it in.

L'Oreal Flash Cat Eye Waterproof All Day Liquid Eyeliner

For those that want a cat eye that stays put — this eyeliner should be your go-to. It comes with a wing stencil for a fail-proof cat eye to help you achieve the perfect wings. The precision tip on this liquid eyeliner also ensures a straight line, while the formula promises a waterproof formula that won't budge.

Maybelline TattooStudio Waterproof, Long Wearing Eyeliner Pencil

The gel eyeliner pencil allows you to achieve a smooth line or a smudgy look — and promises all-day wear. This formula is rich in pigment and has over 12 shades to choose from, from classic black to deep teal. It glides on easily and doesn't fade.

Rimmel Scandaleyes Waterproof Kohl Kajal Liner

Priced under $6, this kohl liner is waterproof, but also a good choice for those with contact lenses or sensitive eyes.
