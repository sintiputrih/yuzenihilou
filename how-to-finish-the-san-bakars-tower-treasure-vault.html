How to Finish the San Bakars Tower Treasure Vault?

Gamers looking out to grab the treasure from this treasure vault will first need to move to the San Bakars Tower Floo Flame, and then go just a little south from there to find the Vault Entrance. Fortunately, Vault Entrances look on the map once the gamer is in the proximity of them, making the procedure of finding them far simpler.

Finishing this puzzle needs gamers to understand Wingardium Leviosa, which is taught by Professor Garlick once her first assignment is finished by utilizing Mandrakes and Venomous Tentacula in combat. In the Vault, gamers will see some big stone blocks with arrows present on them. They are organized in a pattern against the wall, with one block lying on the ground nearby. Gamers should keep in mind that the blocks rotate the direction their arrows point in as they are stacked so that no arrow is directly neighboring to another arrow pointing in the identical direction. Gamers should levitate the loose block, rotating it so that it points downwards to proceed the pattern, and put it into the gap in the blocks against the wall.

Once this is accomplished, the door nearby will slide open, letting gamers to go to the vault’s treasure chest and open it up. Gamers thinking to update and improve their hard won loot might need to look into increasing magical beasts and gathering materials from them, for utilize at the Enchanted Loom to update and enchant gear.
