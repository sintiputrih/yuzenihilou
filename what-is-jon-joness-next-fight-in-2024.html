What is Jon Jones's Next Fight in 2024

Jon Jones, often considered the greatest MMA fighter of all time, is set to make his return to the sport after an injury-induced hiatus. Fans and pundits alike are speculating about the potential opponents for his comeback fight in 2024.

The Injury and Recovery

In October, Jones suffered a pectoral injury that required surgery and an estimated recovery period of eight months.

This setback delayed his anticipated return to the Octagon until the summer or third quarter of 2024.

It's evident that Jones is not likely to return until he is fully recovered from his pectoral injury, ensuring he is in prime condition for his next fight.

Potential Opponents

1. Stipe Miocic

Stipe Miocic, a former UFC heavyweight champion, was originally scheduled to face Jones at UFC 295 before the bout was canceled due to Jones's injury.

The potential matchup between Jones and Miocic remains highly anticipated, and it is expected to be rebooked once Jones is ready to return to the Octagon.

2. Tom Aspinall

Tom Aspinall, the interim heavyweight titleholder, is another intriguing option for Jones's next fight. Aspinall's destructive win over Sergei Pavlovich has solidified his position as one of the best fighters in the heavyweight division.

His impressive performance has sparked interest in a potential showdown with Jones, adding to the excitement surrounding Jones's return to the sport.

3. Curtis Blaydes vs. Jailton Almeida Winner

In the event that matchups against Miocic or Aspinall are not feasible, facing the winner of the Curtis Blaydes vs. Jailton Almeida fight for the lineal heavyweight belt has been suggested as a sensible option for Jones's next fight.

UFC's Plans for Jones's Return

Despite the uncertainties surrounding Jones's next opponent, it is clear that the UFC is keen on rebooking the historic matchup between Jones and Miocic, which was initially slated for UFC 295.

The organization reportedly aims to reschedule this pivotal fight, potentially making it a headline affair in 2024.

This commitment reinforces the significance of Jones's return to the Octagon and sets the stage for a highly anticipated showdown.

Implications and Expectations

The potential matchups for Jones's return carry significant implications for the MMA community.

Whether it's a long-awaited clash with Miocic, a battle against the rising star Aspinall, or a showdown with the winner of an upcoming fight, Jones's next opponent will undoubtedly generate immense interest and excitement within the MMA fanbase.

 

In summation

Jon Jones's next fight in 2024 holds tremendous promise for the MMA world. With potential matchups against Stipe Miocic, Tom Aspinall, or the winner of the Curtis Blaydes vs. Jailton Almeida fight, Jones's return to the Octagon is eagerly anticipated.

The anticipation for his comeback fight reflects his enduring legacy as one of the greatest fighters in MMA history, and fans are eager to witness his next chapter in the sport.
