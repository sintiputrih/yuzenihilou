The AEW WrestleDream 2023 event, held on October 1, 2023, at the Climate Pledge Arena in Seattle, Washington, was a night filled with intense matches, surprising twists, and unforgettable moments.

From title defenses to dream matches, the event delivered on all fronts, leaving fans on the edge of their seats throughout the evening.

Zero Hour Pre-Show

The Zero Hour pre-show set the stage for the electrifying main event with backstage interviews and a captivating clash between Ricky Starks and Wheeler Yuta.

The pre-show also witnessed an intense confrontation involving Christian Cage, Nick Wayne, Sting, and a surprising appearance by WWE Hall of Famer Adam Copeland, also known as Edge.

Match Results

1. Christian Cage vs. Darby Allin

In the highly anticipated 2-out-of-3 Falls Match for the TNT Championship, Christian Cage emerged victorious with a 2-1 win over the resilient Darby Allin, showcasing his unwavering determination and skill in the ring.

2. FTR vs. Aussie Open

FTR secured a hard-fought victory against the formidable Aussie Open, demonstrating their tag team prowess and resilience in a gripping showdown.

3. Bryan Danielson vs. Zack Sabre Jr.

Two wrestling virtuosos, Bryan Danielson and Zack Sabre Jr., engaged in a dream match that captivated the audience. In an exceptional display of technical prowess, Bryan Danielson emerged triumphant, solidifying his status as a force to be reckoned with in the ring.

4. Kris Statlander vs. Julia Hart

In a TBS title defense, Kris Statlander faced off against the determined Julia Hart. Statlander's sheer strength and resilience allowed her to overcome Hart's aggression, securing her title in an intense battle that showcased the prowess of both competitors.

5. Eddie Kingston vs. Katsuyori Shibata

Eddie Kingston's tenacity and skill were on full display as he defended his titles against Katsuyori Shibata. The match, characterized by intense strikes and mutual respect, culminated in Kingston retaining his titles with a powerful powerbomb, leaving the audience in awe of his prowess in the ring.

6. MJF vs. The Righteous

MJF's charisma and in-ring prowess were evident in his handicap match against The Righteous, where he successfully retained the ROH tag titles, captivating the crowd with his entertaining performance.

Other Memorable Matches
