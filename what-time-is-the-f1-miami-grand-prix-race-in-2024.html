Additional Information

Race Track

The Miami International Autodrome, host of the 2024 F1 Miami Grand Prix, is a state-of-the-art racing circuit located in Florida, USA. The track is renowned for its challenging layout, providing a thrilling experience for both drivers and spectators.

The circuit's unique characteristics and strategic layout promise to deliver an electrifying race for all F1 fans.

Previous F1 Races in Miami

The 2024 F1 Miami Grand Prix marks a significant event as the city hosts the prestigious Formula 1 race.

With its rich history in motorsport, Miami has previously witnessed exhilarating F1 races, and the 2024 edition is set to continue this tradition.

The return of F1 to Miami after a hiatus further adds to the anticipation and excitement surrounding the event.

 

The 2024 F1 Miami Grand Prix Race is scheduled to commence at 15:30 local time (EDT) on Sunday, 5th May 2024, and will unfold over the course of either 57 laps or 120 minutes, whichever is shorter.
